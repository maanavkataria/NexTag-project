About NexTag



About Us

NexTag is an online website dedicated to selling premium wallets & purses, featuring collaborations with some of the world’s biggest brands like Louis Vuitton, Michael Kors, Balenciaga, etc. At NexTag, we offer various fashionable and trendy purses & wallets. The website was developed as part of an eProject by a group of students from Aptech Learning, Shahrah-e-Faisal Center, Karachi, from batch AI-202408D1-TTS.

Our Vision

At NexTag, we aspire to be the ultimate destination for luxury purses and wallets, offering an exclusive collection from the world’s top brands. We are committed to delivering authenticity, elegance, and timeless style to our customers. Our vision is to bridge the gap between premium craftsmanship and affordability, making luxury accessible.

Team NexTag

We are a passionate team of four—Maanav Mukesh Kataria, Taha Khalil, Asfar Aziz, and Arsalan Ahmed—who came together to bring this website to life. With a shared vision for innovation and excellence, we combined our skills in design, development, and strategy to create a seamless online experience.
